About This Tutorial

This Tutorial is designed to give you a cursory but effective introduction to graph databases and one of the most popular graph platforms today, Neo4j. We will of course begin with an explanation of what graph databases and Neo4j are. We will also go into detail about the advantages and disadvantages of graph databases by looking at scientific research papers and anylizing their results. As you can imagine graph databases have many tested and proven advantages over other types of databases.

 

Next we will begin the process of actually teaching you how to install Neo4j and begin using it. We will teach all the basic CRUD operations any aspiring Neo4j programmer will need to learn in order to begin using the platform.
